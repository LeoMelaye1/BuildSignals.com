How fast does this start producing appointments?
Most clients see their first appointments within 7 to 14 days of launching. Google campaigns can start generating leads within 48 hours of going live. The qualification and booking process runs continuously from day one.
What does the video warm up actually involve?
You record one short video, 60 seconds to 2 minutes, introducing yourself and your work. We handle the rest. It gets sent automatically to every new lead within minutes of them filling out a form. You record it once and it works forever.
How exactly does the guarantee work?
Before we start, we agree on an appointment target together based on your ad budget and your team's capacity. If we do not hit that number in a given month, you receive a full refund of our service fee for that month. No partial credits. No fine print. Full refund.
Do I need to have my own ad budget on top of your fee?
Yes. Our service fee covers building and managing the system. Ad spend goes directly to Google and Facebook. We recommend a minimum of $1,000 per month in ad spend to generate enough volume to hit your appointment guarantee, though most clients run more.
We have a setter and closer setup. How does this work with our team?
We function as the top of your pipeline. We generate leads, warm them up, and qualify them. You decide whether we book them directly onto your closers' calendars or whether your setters receive them and handle final scheduling. We adapt to whatever workflow your team already uses.
What services do you work with?
We specialize in residential remodeling and home improvement. Bathrooms, kitchens, roofing, windows, patio covers, additions, and similar high ticket residential projects. If your average job value is above $5,000, this system works well for you.
