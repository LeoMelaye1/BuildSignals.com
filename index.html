Before you call

Questions we get every time.

How fast does this start producing booked estimates?
Most contractors see their first appointments within 7 to 14 days of launch. Google campaigns can start pulling leads inside 48 hours of going live, and the qualifying and booking runs from day one.
What does the video warm-up involve?
You record one short video, 60 seconds to a couple minutes, introducing yourself and your work. We can help you film and edit it. After that it sends automatically to every new lead within minutes of their form fill. Record it once, it works forever.
How does the guarantee work?
Before we start, we agree on an appointment number together based on your ad budget and your team's capacity. Miss that number in a given month and you get a full refund of our fee for that month. No partial credits, no fine print.
Do I need my own ad budget on top of your fee?
Yes. Our fee covers building and running the system. Ad spend goes straight to Google and Facebook. We suggest at least $1,000 a month in spend to hit enough volume, though most contractors run more.
We have a setter and closer setup. How does this fit?
We sit at the top of your pipeline. We generate, warm up, and qualify. You decide whether we book straight onto your closers' calendars or hand them to your setters for final scheduling. We fit the workflow you already run.
What kind of work does this fit?
Residential remodeling and home improvement with an average job above about $5,000. Bathrooms, kitchens, roofing, windows, walk-in showers, patio covers, additions, all work well.
