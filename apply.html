What this is

Most college jobs pay $12/hr to stand at a register. This isn't that. BuildSignals books appointments for home remodeling contractors across the US — and we need students who are hungry enough to actually make real money while they're still in school.

Our average setter earns $9,000 a month. You work your own hours, from your dorm or apartment, on a proven script we give you. No experience needed — just the drive to show up and make calls every day.

The long-term play: the best performers move into sales management and build their own team. We'd rather promote from within than hire outside.

You're a fit if

  • You're hungry You're tired of being broke in college and you're willing to do something about it.
  • You're consistent You already do something daily — gym, sports, a side project. You don't need someone pushing you.
  • You're a talker People naturally enjoy your energy. You're warm, easy to talk to, and confident on the phone.
  • You have time You can carve out real hours around your schedule — this isn't a 20-min-a-day thing.
  • You're in the US Must be based in the United States with strong English.
