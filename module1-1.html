Module 1.1 // Mindset

The Identity Shift.

Detailed Breakdown: Why "Telemarketers" fail and "Growth Partners" get rich.

1 The Core Problem: The "BS Detector"

Before you dial a single number, you have to understand the psychology of the person you are calling. We call Blue Collar Business Owners (Contractors). They have a "BS Detector" that is highly sensitive.

Why they hang up instantly:

  • The "Customer Service" Voice: If you pitch your voice up (high frequency), it signals submissiveness. In the construction world, submissiveness = weakness.
  • The Apology: Starting with "Sorry to bother you" confirms that you are, in fact, bothering them. You are admitting guilt before you even speak.
  • The Ask: "Do you have a minute?" signals that you are here to take, not to give.

THE RULE: Contractors do not buy from people they can bully. They buy from people they respect.

3 The Status Game Hierarchy

Every conversation is a negotiation of status. Who is the Alpha in the conversation?

The 3 Levels of Status:

  • Level 1: The Pest (Low Status). Begs for time. Uses "Sir/Madam." Result: Hang up.
  • Level 2: The Vendor (Mid Status). Pitches a product immediately. Result: "Send me an email."
  • Level 3: The Partner (High Status). Audits the business. asks hard questions. Result: A booked meeting.
Low Status Goal
